Ink synthesized in the fragment shader, drawn along animCJK medians

The character 中 is reduced to four centerline polylines (the medians, exactly animCJK's source geometry). No ribbon mesh: every pixel measures its distance to the skeleton, and the brush is a width field thresholded against that distance — corners and caps fall out for free. Texture is domain-warped noise; the wavefront is one scalar per stroke.
